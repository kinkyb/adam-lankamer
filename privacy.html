Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This website is operated by Adam Lankamer, sole trader registered in Spain. This policy explains how we handle personal data collected through adamlankamer.com.

1. Data We Collect

Contact enquiries: If you contact us via the email links on this site, we retain your email address and the content of your message in order to respond to your enquiry.

Technical data: Standard server logs (IP address, browser type, access timestamps) are collected by Netlify, our hosting provider. These are used for security and diagnostics only.

This website does not use contact forms, account registration, or analytics tracking.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To respond to enquiries you send to us by email.
  • To fulfil any translation or photography services you engage us for.
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations.

We do not use your data for marketing, profiling, or any purpose other than responding to your enquiry or delivering requested services.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Netlify — Website hosting. May collect standard server logs per netlify.com/privacy.

We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

4. Data Retention

Email correspondence is retained for as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry or fulfil the agreed service, and for a reasonable period thereafter. You may request deletion at any time.

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EU/EEA, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of personal data we hold about you. You may also lodge a complaint with the Spanish Data Protection Authority (AEPD) at aepd.es.

To exercise any right, email acreatorstore@translatea.com.

6. Cookies

This website does not use tracking, advertising, or analytics cookies. Netlify may set functional cookies as part of hosting infrastructure.

7. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email acreatorstore@translatea.com.
